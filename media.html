Metallohydrolases, designed enzymes for ester hydrolysis

Nobel Lecture 2024 Lead author

Computational design of metal-dependent hydrolases with sophisticated active sites, a step toward custom biocatalysts for green chemistry. Published in Nature (Kim, Woodbury, Ahern et al. 2025).

RFdiffusion2, atom-level enzyme active-site scaffolding

Co-author

A generative model that builds enzyme backbones directly around a catalytic active site, enabling efficient design of enzymes from prompts. Published in Nature Methods (Ahern et al. 2025).
